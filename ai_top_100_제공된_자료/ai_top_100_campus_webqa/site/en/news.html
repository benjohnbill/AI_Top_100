News

Check department news and important announcements.

15 Jan
Academic 2026 Spring Semester Course Registration Guide

Course registration for the 2026 Spring semester will be held from February 10 (Mon) to 14 (Fri). Major required courses can be registered from 10 AM on the first day, and major elective and general courses are available from the second day. Please check the course list on the curriculum page before registration.

10 Jan
Event 16th Programming Competition

The 16th Programming Competition will be held on March 22 (Sat) from 10 AM to 5 PM at Engineering Building B1 Lab. Current undergraduate students are eligible, and teams of 2-3 members can apply. Outstanding teams will receive prize money totaling 3 million won and the Dean's Award.

05 Jan
Scholarship 2026 Spring Scholarship Application

The application period for 2026 Spring university scholarships (academic excellence, study support, work-study) is February 3 (Mon) to February 14 (Fri). Students with a departmental GPA of 3.5 or above are automatically recommended for academic excellence scholarships. Applications and supporting documents can be submitted at the department office or online portal.

28 Dec
Academic Thesis Submission Schedule

The deadline for final thesis submission for February 2026 graduates is January 17 (Fri). Theses must be submitted as PDF files following the department's designated format, accompanied by an approval form signed by the advisor. Submit to the department office (Engineering Building Room 401).

20 Dec
Career 2026 Summer Internship Program

A consolidated recruitment for summer internship programs at major IT companies including Kakao, Naver, and Samsung SDS is underway. The application period is January 6 (Mon) to February 28 (Fri). An internship information session will be held on January 15 (Wed) at 2 PM in the Engineering Building auditorium.

15 Dec
Academic Lab Assignment Results

The 2026 undergraduate research lab assignment results have been finalized. Results can be found on the department website announcements and the office bulletin board. Please visit your assigned lab by January 6 (Mon) to complete a research plan consultation with your advisor.

10 Dec
Event Capstone Design Presentation

The 2025 Fall Capstone Design final presentation will be held on December 18 (Wed) from 10 AM at the Engineering Building auditorium. A total of 24 teams will participate, with industry judges selecting outstanding projects. The presentation is open to all students and visitors — no prior registration required.

05 Dec
Career Job Fair Participation Guide

The 2025 Winter Job Fair will be held on December 12 (Thu) to 13 (Fri) at the Kakao University gymnasium. Over 50 companies from IT, finance, and manufacturing sectors will participate, with on-site interviews and portfolio review booths. Please register in advance through the career support portal and upload your resume.

01 Dec
Event Department Retreat Schedule

The 2025 Winter department retreat will be held from December 20 (Fri) to 21 (Sat) in Pyeongchang, Gangwon-do. The participation fee is 30,000 won per person, due by December 10 (Tue). A chartered bus departs from the school main gate at 8 AM — please gather 10 minutes before departure.

25 Nov
Event 14th Programming Contest Results

The 14th Programming Contest results have been announced. This year's grand prize was awarded to the team of Kim Ji-woo (Junior), Lee Hyung-tae (Junior), and Park Ha-neul (Sophomore). Winner Kim Ji-woo said, "I just coordinated. Planning was led by Ha-neul, and design was led by Hyung-tae. Dividing roles based on each person's strengths was the key."
