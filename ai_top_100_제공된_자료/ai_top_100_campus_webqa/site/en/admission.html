Admission Types

We select talents through various admission tracks

Rolling Admission

Comprehensive Review, Academic Record, SW Talent

  • Comprehensive Review: 25 students
  • Academic Record: 20 students
  • SW Talent: 10 students

* SW Talent: Preference for Olympiad, programming contest winners

Regular Admission

Selection based on CSAT scores

  • Group A: 13 students
  • Group B: 12 students

* CSAT weight: Korean 25%, Math 35%, English 20%, Elective 20%
